Social Science Sans is a font that epitomizes professionalism with a cool, hard edge. 
It is designed for clarity and impact, and its heavy weight makes a statement in any context. 

With its professional demeanor, Social Science Sans speaks with uppercase letters that command attention and ensure readability.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed by Mans Greback, Social Science Sans is a testament to his expertise in creating fonts that meet the demands of contemporary design. This typeface reflects a commitment to functionality without sacrificing style, offering designers a robust tool for creating compelling, professional-grade communications.
